Features

From Zero to First Image

No experience needed. 15 minutes, start to finish.

  1. Download ComfyUI Free, open-source image generation software.
  2. Grab a model from CivitAI Pick one that looks cool. PonyXL, NoobAI, and RealisticPony are great starting points.
  3. Drop the model in ComfyUI's models folder That's the only setup ComfyUI needs.
  4. Open Sigil, type what you want "A girl with red hair in a cherry blossom forest." Plain English.
  5. Send to ComfyUI, get your image Done. You just generated your first AI image. Everything else is optional.

Learn at Your Own Pace

Sigil includes short explanations of SD concepts built right in. Not full tutorials, just enough to know what something does and whether you need it.

What are LoRAs?

Extra training that teaches SD specific things. Like mods for a game. Optional.

What is CFG?

How closely SD follows your prompt. Higher = more literal. 7 is a safe default.

What are samplers?

Different ways SD builds the image. Some faster, some prettier. Euler works fine.

Model compatibility

Pony models need Pony LoRAs. SDXL needs SDXL LoRAs. Not everything mixes.

Prompt weights

(tag:1.2) means "more of this." Default is 1.0. Don't go crazy.

Negative prompts

Tags for things you DON'T want. Bad hands, extra fingers. Common ones are built in.
